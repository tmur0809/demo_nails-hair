About Cool Nails & Hair

We are a boutique salon built around calm, modern beauty and thoughtful service. Our goal is to make each guest feel seen, refreshed, and ready to step out with confidence.

At Cool Nails & Hair, we combine careful technique, premium products, and a relaxed experience to create looks that are elegant without being fussy.

Our team specializes in refined hair styling and clean nail art, meaning every service is tailored to your personal style. Whether you want subtle polish and a polished shape or a fresh haircut with soft movement, we listen closely and deliver results that feel polished and effortless.

We believe the best salons are simple, beautifully curated, and focused on making your visit easy and enjoyable. That means warm service, clear communication, and an environment that feels like a quiet retreat from the busy day.

Book with us to experience professional hair and nail care designed around calm luxury and high-quality results.
